Courses We Offer

Explore our intermediate-level courses designed to enhance your technical skills and knowledge.

Computer Operator and Programming Assistant (COPA)

Develop skills in computer operations, programming languages, database management, and networking. Learn to troubleshoot software and hardware issues and gain proficiency in office productivity tools.

Draughtsman (Civil)

Master the art of creating detailed technical drawings and plans for civil engineering projects. Learn to use computer-aided design (CAD) software and understand construction principles and materials.

Electrician

Get hands-on training in electrical systems, wiring, and installations. Acquire knowledge of electrical safety regulations, circuitry, troubleshooting techniques, and maintenance practices.
